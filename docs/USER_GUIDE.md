# JanVaani User Guide / उपयोग गाइड

This guide is also available inside the app at **`/guide`** (English +
हिन्दी, with a citizen tab and an MP tab). This file is the printable /
linkable version.

---

## For citizens

1. **Sign in & complete your profile.** Sign in and fill your constituency,
   state and pincode once. Your constituency is how your complaint reaches
   the right MP's ledger.
2. **Raise your voice — speak, type, or snap.** On *Raise your voice*
   (`/submit`), record a voice note in your language, type the problem, or
   upload a photo. While typing you'll see **suggested categories** — these
   come from a fixed keyword rule (never an AI guess); tap to accept or pick
   your own.
3. **Check nearby issues.** Once you add a location, a mini-map shows what's
   already reported near you. File yours anyway — repeated reports
   strengthen the evidence.
4. **Drafts are auto-saved** on your device. Come back any time and continue.
5. **Anonymous option.** Toggle *Anonymous* before submitting and your name
   is never attached to the complaint in the MP's view.
6. **Track everything in "My complaints"** (`/my-complaints`): a live
   timeline (Submitted → In progress → Resolved), the assigned department,
   and your complaint's **Evidence strength** with the exact reasons.
7. **Notifications** appear on your profile when the MP's office updates
   your complaint's status.
8. **Civic standing**: 10 points per complaint, 25 more per resolution.
   Badges at fixed thresholds — Voice (10), Advocate (100), Champion (300),
   Guardian (750).

### हिन्दी में संक्षेप
1. साइन इन कर प्रोफ़ाइल पूरी करें — निर्वाचन क्षेत्र ज़रूरी है।
2. `/submit` पर बोलें, लिखें या फोटो लें। श्रेणी के सुझाव तय नियमों से आते हैं।
3. लोकेशन डालते ही आस-पास की दर्ज समस्याएँ दिखती हैं — फिर भी अपनी रिपोर्ट करें।
4. ड्राफ्ट अपने आप सेव होता है।
5. 'गुमनाम' चुनने पर आपका नाम सांसद को कभी नहीं दिखता।
6. `/my-complaints` पर टाइमलाइन, विभाग और प्रमाण की मजबूती (कारणों सहित) देखें।
7. स्थिति बदलने पर प्रोफ़ाइल पर सूचना मिलती है।
8. नागरिक अंक: हर शिकायत पर 10, हल पर 25 और — बैज तय सीमाओं से।

## For MP offices

1. **Access is allowlisted** (admin CLI). Each account is scoped to exactly
   one constituency.
2. **Overview** (`/dashboard`): KPIs, evidence score, ranked issues,
   consensus clusters. Every score's "why" is one click away.
3. **Complaint list**: filter by category/status, play voice notes, view
   photos, move complaints through New → In progress → Resolved, and assign
   a department tag.
4. **"Why this score?"**: click any evidence badge for the stored
   rule-by-rule breakdown.
5. **Compare mode**: last 30 days vs the previous 30 — totals, resolutions,
   per-category deltas.
6. **Notification bell**: new strong-evidence complaints (score ≥ 70) from
   the last 24 hours, refreshed every minute.
7. **Gov data** (`/gov-data`): upload CSV/Excel/PDF or import from
   data.gov.in. Citizen corroboration of a dataset category adds a fixed
   +15 to evidence — data alone never moves a score.
8. **Print report**: an ink-friendly field report of the dashboard, straight
   from the browser.

## The one rule behind every number

**The AI never invents a score, priority, or decision.** Numbers come from
fixed, readable rules (complaint counts, corroboration, attached media,
recency, gov-data matches) and every score ships with its reasons. The AI
only transcribes voice, describes photos, and explains — in your language —
what the rules already decided. The full rulebook is published in-app at
`/about`.
